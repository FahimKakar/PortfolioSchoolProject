Fitness

Growing up through high school I wasn’t always the type of teen that ate food as much, I had an eating disorder that was very unhealthy. I use to eat only once a day and sometimes not even eat at all if I didn’t feel like it. When I started college studying business marketing at the age of 19, my friends motivated and push me to start lifting weights. This started off as a hobby at the start in my first year of college but it turned out to become a lifestyle as I been lifting for 7 years. I learned and made many new friends in my fitness journey that help me get through my ups and downs in life. I learned a lot about nutrition and how our body works with eating certain food. I been doing body building for 6 years but recently started to focus on powerlifting, which is mainly focusing on strength training. I have showed people that its possible change yourself if you put in discipline in your lifestyle and lost 25-30lbs. I have increased my lifts drastically and pulled some impressive numbers. My favorite part of fitness is helping others achieve the same goals because I was once in their position, and seeing other people achieve their goals puts a smile on my face.
